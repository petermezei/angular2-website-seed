Requesting a demo server

To order a Slamby Server please fill the subscription form with your name, your company name, your phone number, email address and select your main business area.

After sending your request, one of our sales agent is going to contact you within a day regarding to your timezone. You can specify which communication channel your prefer to use. After requesting a server we are going to schedule a meeting where we can discuss your usecases and needs. Based on this meeting we can suggest a suitable server size, and SLA construction. Please check the pricing options here.

After ordering a Slamby Server, you can change freely your plan and server sizes monthly, as you see your resource needs.

If you need more information before ordering a server, please schedule a catch up meeting with one of our BI agent.
